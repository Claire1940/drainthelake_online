Progressing in **drain the lake roblox** requires a balance of patience and aggressive upgrading. The lake is divided into several "Depth Zones," each with its own visual style and difficulty level.

**Stage 1: The Shallows (0m - 50m)**
This is the learning phase. Focus on understanding the rhythm of the game. Your goal here is to unlock the first three nodes of the Skill Tree. Don't worry about the phone yet; focus on the "Wooden Bucket" upgrades.

**Stage 2: The Mid-Pool (50m - 200m)**
The water becomes darker, and the distance to the drain increases. This is where **Movement Speed** becomes critical. If you find the walk back to the drain tedious, invest heavily in the Agility branch of the skills.

**Stage 3: The Abyss (200m - 500m)**
Only players with high-tier buckets should venture here. The token yield is massive, but the requirements for collection efficiency are steep. You will likely spend the most time in this stage, grinding for the final set of upgrades.
