<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Guide">

Navigating the depths of the lake requires a structured plan. Rushing into deep water without the necessary upgrades will drastically slow down your token generation rate due to the increased travel time back to the drain. Follow these steps to establish a highly efficient progression pipeline.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Fill Your Bucket">
    Begin by walking to the nearest designated water source. Interact with the water to fill your bucket to its maximum capacity. In the early game, this capacity is very limited, meaning you will need to make frequent trips. Focus on quick, short movements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Drain Water for Tokens">
    Return to the drain point immediately after your bucket is full. Emptying the collected water converts it directly into tokens. These tokens serve as the primary currency for all progression and skill tree advancements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade the Skill Tree">
    Once you have accumulated enough tokens, access the Skill Tree menu. Spend your earnings on upgrades that address the slowest part of your current loop. Typically, increasing your bucket capacity is the best choice for beginners.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Go Deeper">
    As your stats improve, venture further into the lake. Deeper zones yield higher token multipliers but require longer travel times. Only transition to a deeper zone when your bucket speed and capacity can support the extra distance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Repeat the Loop">
    Continue repeating this cycle, gradually unlocking deeper water levels and upgrading your gear until you finally reach the bottom to recover your lost phone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To help you plan your journey, the table below highlights the requirements and multipliers for the primary depth zones available in the game.

| Zone | Water Depth | Min Bucket Level | Token Multiplier |
| **Shallows** | 0 to 10 meters | Level 1 | 1.0x |
| **Mid-Deep** | 11 to 30 meters | Level 3 | 1.5x |
| **Abyssal** | 31 meters plus | Level 6 | 2.5x |

Managing your depth transitions is key. If you notice your tokens-per-minute rate dropping after moving to a deeper zone, return to the previous zone and upgrade your capacity further.

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Advice">
Do not move to the Mid-Deep zone until you have upgraded your bucket capacity to at least level 3. The travel time will otherwise diminish your earnings.
</Callout>
