<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Core Gameplay Loop and Mechanics">

Drain the Lake is an incremental Roblox experience where your primary objective is to recover a lost phone resting at the bottom of a deep lake. To achieve this, you must systematically empty the lake of its water, earn tokens, upgrade your gear, and unlock deeper zones. The gameplay relies heavily on repeating a highly optimized loop of gathering water, draining it, and spending the currency on permanent upgrades.

Understanding the mechanics of water collection is crucial for early success. When you start the game, you are equipped with a basic bucket that has limited capacity. Walking to the water source fills your bucket automatically. Once full, you must return to the central drain point to deposit the water. Draining water converts it directly into tokens, which are used to unlock nodes on your skill tree.

<Callout type="warning" title="Progression Trap">
Do not attempt to explore deep areas immediately. Walking far distances with a low-capacity bucket decreases your token-per-minute efficiency significantly.
</Callout>

To help you visualize the basic structure of the loop, review the table below outlining each phase of the core gameplay loop and its primary metric.

|Phase|Action|Primary Reward|Key Metric|
|---|---|---|---|
|**Gathering**|Fill bucket at water source|Water volume|Fill speed|
|**Draining**|Empty bucket at drain point|**Tokens**|Drain efficiency|
|**Upgrading**|Spend tokens on Skill Tree|Permanent buffs|Token cost|
|**Exploring**|Descend to deeper areas|New zones|Depth reached|

Each cycle of this loop increases your capability to handle larger volumes of water. As you gain more tokens, the distance you can cover increases, allowing you to venture deeper into the lakebed without sacrificing your earning potential.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Skill Tree Upgrade Priorities">

The skill tree is the foundation of long-term progression in the game. Spending your tokens randomly will slow down your progress. Instead, you must focus on upgrading specific branches of the skill tree that directly reduce the time it takes to complete a single run. The tree is divided into capacity, collection speed, token multipliers, and depth access.

To maximize your efficiency, you should prioritize upgrades that resolve your immediate bottlenecks. For example, if you find yourself spending more time walking than filling, your capacity is too low. Conversely, if you fill your bucket instantly but earn very few tokens, you need to invest in token efficiency.

<Callout type="tip" title="Upgrade Strategy">
Always prioritize Bucket Capacity over Collection Efficiency in the early game. Carrying more water per trip is more valuable than filling a tiny bucket faster.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Token Farming Optimization">

Farming tokens efficiently is the key to completing the game quickly. The speed at which you earn tokens is determined by your route selection, movement speed, and bucket capacity. Experienced players design specific farming routes that minimize downtime and maximize the volume of water delivered to the drain per minute.

When farming, always look for the shortest path between the water edge and the drain. Avoid taking detours or jumping over unnecessary obstacles, as these actions waste valuable seconds. Over long play sessions, saving even two seconds per trip can result in thousands of extra tokens.

<Callout type="info" title="Farming Tip">
If a new zone is too far away, it is often more efficient to farm the previous zone faster until you can afford a movement speed or capacity upgrade.
</Callout>
