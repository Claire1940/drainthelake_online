<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Early to Late Game Progression">

Reaching the bottom of the lake requires a systematic approach. Follow this structured progression path to optimize your journey from a beginner with a basic wooden bucket to an advanced explorer ready to recover the lost phone.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Basic Loop">
    Begin by running the shortest possible route between the surface water and the drain. Do not attempt to explore deeper yet. Focus entirely on securing your first few hundred tokens.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Eliminate Early Bottlenecks">
    Spend your initial tokens on Bucket Capacity and Collection Efficiency. This reduces the friction of your starter setup and makes each run feel significantly smoother.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Boost Token Multiplication">
    Once your basic loop is fast, invest heavily in Token Efficiency. This ensures that every bucket of water you drain yields substantially more resources, preparing you for expensive mid-game upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock the Mid-Depth Areas">
    With an upgraded bucket and boosted token rates, unlock the next depth level. Establish a new, optimized route in this area and adapt to the increased travel distance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final Descent">
    Maximize your remaining Skill Tree nodes. Once your capacity and speed can comfortably support long-distance travel, push to the deepest point of the lake to recover your lost phone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Rushing">
Do not unlock deeper areas too early. If your movement speed or bucket capacity is too low, the long travel times will actually lower your overall tokens-per-minute ratio.
</Callout>
