To successfully complete a **drain the lake walkthrough**, you must understand the underlying incremental mechanics of this popular 2026 Roblox experience. | Phase | Core Action | Primary Benefit |

  <Step num="2" title="Invest in Capacity and Speed">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Acquire Upgraded Buckets">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Automate the Drain Loop">
    As you unlock deeper tiers of the Skill Tree, focus on automation nodes. These upgrades allow you to generate passive token income and streamline the bailing process, allowing you to focus on exploration.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Reach the Deep Lake and Retrieve the Phone">
    Once your capacity, movement speed, and token multipliers are sufficiently upgraded, follow the deepest path down the lake bed. Navigate past the final drainage thresholds to locate and recover your lost phone, successfully completing the walkthrough.

</Steps>
