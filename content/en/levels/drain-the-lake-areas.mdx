<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Area Progression">

Unlocking deeper **drain the lake areas** requires a disciplined approach. Rushing into deep water without the necessary upgrades will result in very low token-per-minute yields. Follow this structured path to reach the bottom of the lake as quickly as possible.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Shoreline Grind">
    Start at the very edge of the water. Do not swim out. Fill your bucket at the nearest water point and drain it immediately. Repeat this until you can afford the first two Bucket Capacity upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Expanding the Loop">
    Move into the Sunlit Shelf (50m+). Here, the water is deeper, and the tokens per bucket are slightly higher. Focus on balancing your Token Multiplier and Movement Speed to keep your loop time under 20 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Entering the Twilight Zone">
    Once you reach the 200m mark, travel time becomes a major factor. You must have at least Tier 2 Movement Speed. Start looking for "Treasure" items that occasionally appear in this area for massive token bonuses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Deep Sea Push">
    At 500m+, the environment darkens. Use your upgrades to maintain a consistent rhythm. Do not get distracted by exploring the edges; stay on the direct path between the deepest unlocked point and the drain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Phone Recovery">
    In the final Abyss area, your bucket capacity should be at maximum. The goal here is to locate the lost phone. Once found, a final massive drain cycle is required to complete the main objective.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Rushing">
Entering the Midnight Floor with Tier 1 Capacity is a common mistake. You will spend 90% of your time walking and only 10% actually earning tokens, which stalls your progress.
</Callout>

</Section>
