Drain the Lake is a popular incremental adventure on Roblox where players are tasked with recovering their lost phone from the bottom of a massive body of water. The primary upgrade branches include Bucket Capacity, Collection Efficiency, Token Efficiency, and Depth Progression. Balancing these upgrades ensures that your token income keeps pace with the increasing travel distances of deeper zones.

During the early stages of the game, your primary limitation is the amount of water you can carry. Upgrading your bucket capacity should be your top priority, followed closely by collection speed. Once you can fill and empty a larger bucket quickly, you can start investing in token efficiency to multiply your earnings. Depth progression should only be targeted when you can comfortably clear the current level.
