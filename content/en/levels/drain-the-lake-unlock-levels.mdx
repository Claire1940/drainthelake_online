<Section icon={Map} title="How to Unlock Levels in Drain the Lake">

In the Roblox incremental hit *Drain the Lake*, progression is directly tied to how deep you can go. You cannot simply swim to the bottom of the lake from the start; the crushing depth and distance will reset your character if you are unprepared. To unlock deeper levels, you must systematically invest in your **Skill Tree** using tokens earned from draining water.

As you drain water and earn tokens, you unlock access to deeper checkpoints. Each new level features higher-value resources, such as chests and rare gems, which increase your token generation exponentially.

| Level / Zone | Token Requirement | Key Features & Hazards | Unlock Priority |
|--- |--- |--- |--- |
| **Surface Zone** | **0 Tokens** | Shallow water, tutorial drain, basic chests | Starting Area |
| **Twilight Zone** | **500 Tokens** | Faster water accumulation, basic checkpoints | High |
| **Midnight Zone** | **2,500 Tokens** | Deep trenches, high-yield mineral deposits | Medium |
| **Abyssal Zone** | **10,000 Tokens** | Extreme depth, Volcano, Lost Phone objective | Ultimate Goal |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Do not try to rush into deeper levels before upgrading your bucket capacity. The long walk back to the drain pipe will severely slow down your token-per-minute ratio.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Level Unlocking Guide">

Follow this systematic progression route to efficiently drain the lake, unlock every single depth level, and ultimately recover your lost phone.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Surface Loop">
    Equip your starting bucket and scoop water from the shallow shoreline. Dump it directly into the drain pipe to secure your first batch of tokens. Repeat this loop until you can afford the first two levels of **Bucket Capacity**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock the Twilight Checkpoint">
    Purchase the Twilight Depth node in your Skill Tree. Swim down to activate the first underwater checkpoint. This allows you to dump water closer to the source rather than walking all the way back to the surface drain.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm Underwater Minerals">
    Once in the Midnight Zone, stop focusing solely on water. Search the lakebed for glowing mineral chests and gems. These give massive flat token bonuses that bypass the standard bailing limits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reach the Abyssal Volcano">
    Accumulate 10,000 tokens to unlock the final Abyssal Zone. Navigate past the underwater volcano and locate your lost phone at the absolute bottom of the lake to complete the main game progression.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success">
Once you unlock the **Direct Deposit** node in the late-game Skill Tree, you can stand near deep water sources and automatically earn tokens, making the final push to the Abyss much easier.
</Callout>

</Section>
