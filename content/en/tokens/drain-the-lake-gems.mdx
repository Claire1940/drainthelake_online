<Section icon={Trophy} title="Farming Drain the Lake Gems: Best Methods">

Acquiring **drain the lake gems** is your top priority for long-term progression. Unlike tokens, which vanish when you leave or lose a match, gems remain in your inventory. This makes them the ultimate resource for buying better buckets at the lobby shop. To build a steady supply of gems, you must focus on two primary sources: opening chests during active runs and claiming badge achievements in the main lobby.

Chests spawn randomly around the lake as you drain the water. Whenever you spot one, prioritize opening it immediately. Badges, on the other hand, function as milestone achievements. You can track your progress and claim your rewards at the dedicated badges station.

| Badge Name | Requirement | Gem Reward | Special Unlock |

| **Lake Sweeper** | Reach checkpoint 3 | **100 Gems** | None |
| **Badge Master** | Collect 10 badges | **500 Gems** | Ancient Bucket |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Run Upgrades & Progression Strategy">

Every new run starts from scratch in the lobby. To make each attempt successful, you must spend your earned tokens wisely at the upgrade computer. While it might be tempting to focus solely on bucket capacity, prioritizing your character's stats will yield better results.

Optimizing your upgrade path ensures that you can gather tokens quickly, which in turn helps you survive longer and reach deeper checkpoints. Checkpoints act as respawn points and are indicated by flag masts in the water.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Movement Speed">
    Buy movement speed upgrades first. Faster travel between the lake and the drain station increases your token-per-minute rate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Increase Bucket Capacity">
    Upgrade capacity once you can move quickly. This reduces the number of trips required to drain large amounts of water.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Boost Fill Speed">
    Invest in fill speed to minimize the time spent standing near the water, lowering your exposure to hazards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Upgrade Balance">
Keep your movement speed at least two levels higher than your bucket capacity to maintain a fast and efficient farming rhythm.
</Callout>

|-|-|-|-|
| **Movement Speed** | **High** | Moderate | Reduces travel time |
| **Bucket Capacity** | **Medium** | High | Increases water per trip |
| **Fill Speed** | **Low** | Low | Reduces clicking time |
