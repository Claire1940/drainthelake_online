To maximize your **drain the lake rewards**, you need a highly optimized farming route. Rushing blindly into deep water will only slow you down due to the increased travel time back to the drain pipe. Follow this step-by-step process to establish a highly profitable loop.

  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Shortest Route">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize Bucket Fills">

  <Step num="3" title="Drain and Reinvest">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Hunt for Treasure Chests">
