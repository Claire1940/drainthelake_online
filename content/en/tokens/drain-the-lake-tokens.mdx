<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding the Token Progression Loop">

The core gameplay loop of Roblox's Drain the Lake revolves around a simple cycle: fill your bucket, transport the water, and empty it at the drain point to earn **drain the lake tokens**. These tokens serve as the main currency required to unlock critical Skill Tree upgrades, which in turn allow you to venture deeper into the lake and recover your lost phone.

If you ignore efficiency, the grind can feel slow. To make steady progress, you must balance your current bucket capacity with your collection speed. Spending too much time walking back and forth with a small bucket is the most common mistake new players make.

| Progression Phase | Primary Target | Token Focus | Strategy Objective |

| **Early Game** | Shallow Water | Capacity & Speed | Minimize travel times |
| **Mid Game** | Mid-Depth Slopes | Token Multipliers | Build up passive skill bonuses |
| **Late Game** | Deep Abyss | Depth Progression | Secure the lost phone |

<Callout type="tip" title="Farming Tip">

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Skill Tree Upgrade Priorities">

The Skill Tree is where you spend your hard-earned **drain the lake tokens** to permanently boost your character's capabilities. Because resources are limited in the early stages, following a structured upgrade path will save you hours of unnecessary grinding.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Boost Bucket Capacity First">
    Increase your capacity to at least level 5. This reduces the sheer volume of trips you need to make between the water and the drain, giving you a solid foundation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Invest in Token Efficiency">
    Once you can carry a decent amount of water, unlock the Token Efficiency nodes. This ensures that every single drop of water you empty yields more tokens.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Upgrade Collection Speed">
    With a larger bucket, filling can start to feel slow. Upgrade your collection efficiency to vacuum up water in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Depth and Mobility">
    After optimizing your economy, invest in depth progression and movement speed to begin your descent toward the bottom of the lake.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Recommended Build Order">
