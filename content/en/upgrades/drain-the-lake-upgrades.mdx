<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Core Mechanics of Drain the Lake Upgrades">

The progression loop in [Drain the Lake on Roblox](https://www.roblox.com/games/138381251771774/Drain-the-Lake) is straightforward but highly satisfying. Your ultimate goal is to recover your lost phone from the bottom of the lake. To accomplish this, you must scoop water into your bucket, dump it at the drain point to earn tokens, and reinvest those tokens back into your Skill Tree. 

Understanding which upgrades to purchase first determines how quickly you can scale your operation. Investing randomly can lead to severe progression bottlenecks where you spend more time walking back and forth than actually clearing water.

| Upgrade Type | Primary Benefit | Progression Phase | Impact Rating |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Bucket Capacity** | Carries more water per run, reducing back-and-forth trips | Early Game | ★★★★★ |
| **Bail Speed** | Increases the rate at which you gather water from the lake | Early to Mid Game | ★★★★☆ |
| **Token Multiplier** | Multiplies the tokens earned from bailing water | Mid Game | ★★★★★ |
| **Automation Nodes** | Automates the collection and draining processes for idle play | Late Game | ★★★★★ |
| **Depth Progression** | Grants access to deeper areas containing higher-value water | Late Game | ★★★★☆ |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Always balance your Bucket Capacity with your Bail Speed. Having a massive bucket is useless if it takes you ages to fill it up, and high speed is wasted if you must run back to the drain point every three seconds.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Progression Guide">

To transition efficiently from a beginner with a wooden bucket to an automated draining machine, follow these structured steps:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Early Loop">
    Begin by running the shortest possible route between the water source and the drain point. Focus all your initial tokens on upgrading your basic bucket capacity to level 5 and bail speed to level 3.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Token Multipliers">
    Once your bucket can hold a decent amount of water, invest heavily in the Token Multiplier upgrades. This ensures that your back-and-forth trips are rewarded with significantly larger payouts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secure Automation Nodes">
    Save your tokens to unlock the first automation nodes in the Skill Tree. This allows your character to automatically scoop and empty water, transforming the game into an efficient idle simulator.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Push for Deeper Descents">
    With automation running, use your massive token reserves to unlock deeper lake sections. Deeper water provides much higher token yields, compounding your passive income.
  </Step>
</Steps>
